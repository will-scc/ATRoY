The sewers are large labyrinth entered from Aston square ( Green Dot in 1,5,9,13 junction at center of map ) .  There are 16 sections. The Ruby Red Dot  at (69,192) in the junction of sections 13,14,15,16, is of course Ruby, she needs G, H & I shrooms. (Sets of G,H&I are occasionally sold in Aston Sq for about 300g)  Does she ever do anything for herself ?  The Ratlings (Purple) will tell you ( if you are level 13 or higher) where to look for treasure when you kill them.  Remember this, directions refer to whole sewer not just section you are in,  use the map and check every grate ( Blue ) North is the 0,0 corner. The Red blocks within sections 6 & 14 are open to look across but cannot be passed.  There are Amulets and Rings here of great power as well as many gold coins, no doubt lost down the drains by the mages living above, one by the name of Rodney appears to have been particularly careless..   Hmmm  Could that be the Wizard of Yendor.


When exploring the sewers it is very wise to bring a recall scroll.  You can easily loose your bearings and it can be a long and dangerous walk out.  

For those of you in New World, Urd.V3.5 there are experience poles down here. ☺
