Bonus Prize...+5 Orbs upon completing ALL RDs

You have 30 real life min to complete an RD, after that it collapses, you will be ejected unharmed back at the entrance.

The Skellies in the RD's are stronger than their level would indicate,( about +3) exercise caution.

When you complete an RD you have 4 choices,

1) wait for it to collapse,   2) Try and walk back to entrance,    3) Use a recall scroll,     4) Lag out.

If you die in an RD simply tell the attendant to destroy the RD and your body will be ejected complete with your possessions for you to loot. (See V3 Secure TX Method on FAQ Page)  V3 RD's are split in 2 mirrors.

WELDING SHRINE MINIMUM LEVELS:  The formula for determining the highest weld shrine that your character is capable os using is CHARACTER_LEVEL * 1.25 + 2. For example, if your level is 22 then 22 * 1.25 + 2 = 29.5 which rounds up to 30. However, since the highest weld shrine which is equal to or less than 30 is 27, then the highest weld shrine you are capable of using is in RD27.

LEVEL LIMITS:  When you create a RD there are limits imposed of plus or minus 3 Levels(This was expanded to plus or minus 5 levels in A3RES).   This is to prevent high levels escorting.  Thus if a lvl 20 creates a RD then only levels 17-23 may enter it. Thus max escort possible is if you have someone 3 levels higher than you create the RD,   E.g. You Lvl 17,  RD Creator Lvl 20, Max Escort Lvl 23

Continuity Continuity Shrines (Shown here highlighted) must be visited in sequence 10 thru 99.  If you miss one, the next shrine will say that you must visit it's younger brother first.
Bribery Bribery Shrines will take your gold up to the maximum amount shown in return for experience...        Be wise leave some in the bank. However if you don't have enough the shrine will laugh at you.
Security Security Shrines give you a save if you have 5 or less, save them for when you need them. They don't work for hardcore players,   "Thou shalt never be secure"
Living On The Edge L.O.E Shrines will take ALL your saves and give you experience. If you are HC it just tells you  "Thou art already living on the edge"
Indecisiveness Indecisiveness Shrines take 3  points from each stat and give you back the experience allowing you to reallocate it, just be sure to raise your weapon stat back so you can still use that  Ruby weapon you're carrying. Do NOT use if you have Negs. It messes up your stats.
Jobless

Jobless: "You're Fired !" removes ALL professions, you get the PPoints back, but not the gold it took to learn them.

( THIS IS NOT THE SAME AS A JOBLESS / FORGETFULNESS POTION)
Welding

Have only the 2 items you want to weld equipped. Like will not weld to like.  Inventory is safe. PLAN your welds, try only to weld high stat items, +8 or better, The movement is random unless you use Orbs.

"You can always use weld shrines below your current level, but not all above your level (cap ranges from +5 to +12). " See FAQ on Welding
Death

Death in RD37 kills you but... you get the title The Brave after your name, be sure and use LOE shrine in RD 36  first to avoid too many negs

( Yes it kills all your saves too giving extra negs for each one)
Bravery Bravery in RD51, which can only be used if Death was used, gives you mucho experience
Kindness Kindness in RD's 21 & 55 removes Player killer status instantly,  if you're not PK it tells you "Thou art already Kind"
Vitality

For a Mage:  You get Mana + 5    For a Warrior: You get  HP + 5

This is capped at 100/115
